Meet Our Agents

Romeo Agresti Fabrizio Romano Mohamed Bouhafsi
I'm a seasoned Juventus correspondent now with TransferZone, previously contributing to Calcio GP and serving as a commentator for Juventus Channel. My expertise spans from on-pitch analysis to in-depth coverage of the transfer market. In 2012, I published "The Great History of Derbies: Turin-Juventus" and was honored with the "Calciomercato d'Or" award in 2017 for my contributions to football journalism. Fabrizio Romano, an Italian sports journalist renowned for his expertise in football transfers, gained prominence with his trademark catchphrase "Here we go!" when announcing deals. He began his football writing journey in 2009 during high school and transitioned to transfer journalism in 2011. Since joining Sky Sport Italy in 2012, he has cultivated extensive contacts with clubs, agents, and intermediaries across Europe. Romano also contributes as a reporter for The Guardian and CBS Sports. Mohamed Bouhafsi's meteoric rise in sports journalism is notable despite his youth. Graduating from the European Institute of Journalism in 2012, he swiftly progressed from internships at Canal Plus and Radio France to football editor-in-chief roles at RMC and BFM TV. Responsible for football content across major platforms, Bouhafsi also addresses social issues. Recognized as a future star by Le Figaro in 2014, he's esteemed as a tier 1 journalist. Joining "TransferZone" as its newest agent recruit, Bouhafsi's achievements underscore his talent and dedication.
